Rules of Nine Men's Morris:
  1. Each player has 9 pieces when the game begins.
  2. Unused pieces are placed in the players drawers.
  3. When player has pieces in the drawer then he can only place new pieces on empty fields.
  4. When player has no pieces left in the drawer then he can move one of his pieces to neighbouring empty field.
  5. When player has no pieces left in the drawer and has 3 pieces on board then he can move one of his pieces to any empty field.
  6. After selecting piece for moving available places to move are highlighted.
  7. If placed or moved piece created mill (3 of player pieces connected in straight line) then player can remove one of opponent pieces from board.
  8. Last used player position is not available in the next move.
  9. Game ends when player has less than 3 pieces left or when he can't make any move.
